Methodology

How It Works

Our approach is based on the Weighted Decision Matrix — a proven analytical framework.

The Weighted Decision Matrix helps you evaluate multiple options against criteria that matter to you. Each factor gets an importance weight, and each option gets a score per factor.

  • Identify your options
  • Define evaluation factors
  • Assign importance weights (1-10)
  • Score each option per factor (1-10)
  • Calculate weighted scores
  • Compare and decide

The Formula

Final Score = Σ ( Factor Weight × Option Rating )

Each option's total score is the sum of its ratings multiplied by each factor's importance weight. Scores are then normalized to a 0–100% scale for easy comparison across options with different numbers of factors.
